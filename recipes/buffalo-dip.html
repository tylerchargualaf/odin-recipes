Buffalo Chicken Dip

Dip Recipe

 Looking for a quick snack? A party food favorite? A great food addition to Saturday night football? Look no further. This buffalo chicken dip recipe will have your mouth watering in under 15 min!

Ingredient List
  • 1 Block Cream Cheese
  • 1/2 cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • 1/2 cup Ranch
  • 2/3 cup Frank's Hot Sauce
  • 1 Can Shredded Chicken

Steps
  1. In a pot, add ranch and frank's hot sauce
  2. In the same pot, mix the shredded chicken and cheese together with the wet ingredients
  3. Add block of cream cheese to the pot and turn stove on to medium heat
  4. Stir until cream cheese is completely mixed and enjoy!

Pro Tip: Serve with tortilla chips!

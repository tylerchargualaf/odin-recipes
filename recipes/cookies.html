Soft-Batch Chocolate Chip Cookies

Cookie Recipe

 This recipe contains the exact ingredients needed to create the best chocolate chip cookie you have ever had! No funny ingredients, crazy prepping or anything of the sort. The only thing standing between you and these cookies is 20 minutes of time!

Ingredient List
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 cup Flour
  • 3 tbsp Avocado Oil
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/2 cup White Sugar
  • 1/4 cup Brown Sugar
  • 1 cup Chocolate Chips

Steps
  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Add avacado oil, egg, white sugar, and brown sugar to a bowl
  3. Stir ingredients until mixed
  4. In a seperate bowl, add flour, chocolate chips and baking soda
  5. Mix wet and dry ingredients together and add water as needed to the dough
  6. Roll golf-ball sized balls and place on baking sheet
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes and enjoy!

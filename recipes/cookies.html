Soft-Batch Chocolate Chip Cookies

Cookie Recipe

This recipe contains the exact ingredients needed to create the best chocolate chip cookie you have ever had! No funny ingredients, crazy prepping or anything of the sort. The only thing standing between you and these cookies is 15 minutes of time!

Ingredient List
  • Avocado Oil
  • Baking Soda
  • White Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Flour
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps
  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Add 3 tbsp avacado oil, 1 egg, 1/2 Cup Sugar, 1/4 Cup Brown Sugar to a bowl and stir until mixed
  3. In a seperate bowl add 1 1/2 Cup Flour, 1 Cup chocolate chips 1/2 tsp baking soda
  4. Mix wet and dry ingredients together and add water as needed as dough becomes solid
  5. Roll golf ball sized doughballs and place on baking sheet
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes and enjoy!
